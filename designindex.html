Prototype | PDF

This project focused on creating greater civic engagement between San Francisco Arts Commission & the San Francisco population.


Report

PDF

The motivation behind this project was a passion for human rights. Having a background in International Development & Tech, there was a realization that technologies can be used to help bring awareness to the abuses that happen in places where voices are diminished & silenced. The purpose behind this was to provide a safe space where people can come to report those abuses. As well as connect them with media & people who can help these areas of conflict.
