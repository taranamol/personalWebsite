summary

The design process for Veteran’s Affairs’ Mood Coach was dictated by our user’s needs. We learned that Mood Coach’s target user is someone who lives with depression, has trouble staying motivated with everyday responsibilities, and believes in the power of self-help. Our solution is designed to address the needs of this target population by staying motivated during the toughest moments of depression. It features the ability to set daily tasks & goals with notifications allowing us to meet the user’s need of accountability.
