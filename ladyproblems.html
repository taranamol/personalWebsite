design research & synthesis

Brainstroming & Affinity Map

We started the research process by brainstroming pain points women face in the workplace. Second, we did an affinity map to understand the prevelent themes within the pain points.











User Interviews

We validated the challenges by interviewing various women who work in different industries. This also helped us narrow down the scope of our primary users & dig deeper into workplace culture.

After our first round of user interviews, we learned women face a range of problems which stem from:

  • Patriarchal society
  • Stereotypical female/male-centric way of thinking
  • Lack of counsel & knowledge in knowing how to maneuver around work politics
  • Lack of confidence because they don't feel supported

In the second round of interviews, we explored different support groups women have. Our goal was to understand their perspective on accountability/support & community. We spoke with women to understand the who, the why, the how, what has worked, what can be important.

target audience

target user

Women who are just starting their career out of college.

issues they face

They are unaware how to navigate through work politics, negotiation, & finance. Often times, they don't know where to start or what actions they can take to improve their career.

core problem

Women lack confidence in the workplace. One of the reasons they feel this way is because they lack inside knowledge as a working professional.

solution process

Solution Statement

Give women confidence by informing them of opportunities.
